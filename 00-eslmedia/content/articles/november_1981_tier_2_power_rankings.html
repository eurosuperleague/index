Europa League Power Rankings: Sheffield grabs the top line

Sheffield United and Marseille are both winning at promotion pace, while Benfica and Chelsea keep enough talent close behind to make Tier 2 tense.

Nina ValeNovember 1981 power board

Tier 2 has two obvious leaders and one important tie-breaker. Sheffield United and Marseille are both 6-1, both on long winning streaks, and both already look like promotion-quality teams. Sheffield takes the top line because the margin is louder: plus-120 overall, a 42-point win over Aston Villa, and a five-game streak that has turned strong into ruthless.

Europa League Board
  1. 1Sheffield United
    6-1 | +120 | Rickey Green
    The strongest point differential in the tier gives Sheffield the top line. The current W5 streak is not subtle.
  2. 2Marseille
    6-1 | +87 | Robert Parish
    Marseille is nearly co-No. 1. Four straight wins and a plus-87 margin would lead most months.
  3. 3Benfica
    5-2 | +22 | Moses Malone
    Benfica is the chaser with the cleanest record. The margin is not elite, but the win total keeps pressure on the top two.
  4. 4Chelsea
    4-3 | +63 | Jack Sikma
    Chelsea is the team I am still unsure about. The differential says contender; the record says the mistakes have mattered.
  5. 5Juventus
    3-3 | +26 | Kareem Abdul-Jabbar
    Juventus has enough positive margin to stay above the bottom tier, but the .500 record limits the argument.
  6. 6Ajax
    2-4 | -74 | Cliff Robinson
    Ajax is the faller. Four straight losses have turned a survivable start into a problem.
  7. 7Monaco
    1-5 | -124 | Purvis Short
    Monaco's margin is worse than Aston Villa's, which keeps this from feeling safe despite the extra win.
  8. 8Aston Villa
    0-8 | -120 | Bill Laimbeer
    No wins, eight losses, and no serious counterargument. Villa is last until it earns a different sentence.
The Promotion Shape

The important part is that Tier 2 already has a proper top four. Sheffield and Marseille are setting the pace, Benfica is one result away from making this uncomfortable, and Chelsea's differential says the club is better than its record. That is a useful race, not a settled table.
