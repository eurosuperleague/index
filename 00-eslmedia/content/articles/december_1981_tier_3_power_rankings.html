Conference League Power Rankings: FL Fart Holds, Inter Pushes

Tier 3 is no longer Barcelona's board; FL Fart has the record edge, Inter Milan has the margin case, and Sporting CP has momentum.

Nina ValeDecember 1981 power board

The November board gave Barcelona the benefit of the record. December takes that benefit away. Barcelona went 2-4, FL Fart held the best full-season record at 9-5, and Inter Milan built the most convincing point-differential case in the tier. With only one promotion spot available, the order has to reward the team that has done the most across the full file while still respecting who is moving fastest.

Conference League Board - Movement From November
  1. 1FL Fart
    Up 1 | 9-5 | +41 | Alton Lister
    The latest sim was not dominant, but the overall record is still the cleanest. FL Fart gets first with a warning label.
  2. 2Inter Milan
    Up 1 | 8-6 | +86 | World B. Free
    Inter owns the strongest margin case in Tier 3 after a plus-49 December. If this were purely underlying quality, they might already be first.
  3. 3Valencia
    Up 1 | 8-6 | +7 | Isiah Thomas
    Valencia keeps winning enough to stay in the race, even if the point differential still asks for caution.
  4. 4Barcelona
    Down 3 | 8-5 | +4 | Kelly Tripucka
    This is the faller. Barcelona still has the record to argue higher, but a 2-4 month changes the trust level.
  5. 5Sporting CP
    Up 2 | 7-8 | -14 | Buck Williams
    Sporting is the riser. A 5-3 month puts them on the live promotion line in the race snapshot, even if the full-season margin is still negative.
  6. 6Paris Saint-Germain
    Down 1 | 7-8 | +22 | Joe Barry Carroll
    PSG's margin says they are better than sixth. The record and Sporting's momentum say this is where they sit today.
  7. 7Manchester City
    Down 1 | 5-9 | -48 | Allen Leavell
    City has not collapsed, but the standings gap is now real. Reputation is doing too much work.
  8. 8Tottenham Hotspur
    No change | 5-10 | -98 | John Long
    Tottenham beat Barcelona by one, which gives the month a useful moment. The full record still gives them last.
The One-Spot Problem

FL Fart, Inter Milan, Valencia, Barcelona, and Sporting CP can all tell a plausible story. That is the problem and the charm. In a one-promotion tier, the next bad week will feel twice as expensive.
