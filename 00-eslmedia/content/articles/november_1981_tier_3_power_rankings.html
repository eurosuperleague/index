Conference League Power Rankings: Barcelona leads a strange board

Barcelona has the best record in Tier 3, but FL Fart's margin and Inter Milan's efficiency keep this board from feeling settled.

Nina ValeNovember 1981 power board

Tier 3 is the strangest board because the best record and best underlying case do not belong to the same team. Barcelona is 6-1, which matters most in a one-promotion format. FL Fart is 5-2 with a plus-53 differential, which is the loudest performance profile in the tier. The choice is close. The standings edge goes to Barcelona for now.

Conference League Board
  1. 1Barcelona
    6-1 | +16 | Kelly Tripucka
    The record is the headline. Barcelona is not dominating margins, but 6-1 is still the cleanest promotion claim.
  2. 2FL Fart
    5-2 | +53 | Alton Lister
    This is the team I am least sure should be second. The differential says first, but the extra loss keeps them just below Barcelona.
  3. 3Inter Milan
    4-2 | +37 | World B. Free
    Inter has played fewer games than some rivals, but the efficiency profile is strong enough to stay in the promotion conversation.
  4. 4Valencia
    4-3 | -4 | Isiah Thomas
    Valencia's record is useful, but the negative margin says every win has been doing extra work.
  5. 5Paris Saint-Germain
    3-4 | +21 | Joe Barry Carroll
    PSG is better than its record. The positive margin keeps them above the lower group.
  6. 6Manchester City
    2-4 | -24 | Allen Leavell
    City is the faller. The name still carries reputation, but November's file has been poor.
  7. 7Sporting CP
    2-5 | -30 | Buck Williams
    Sporting has not been far from City, but the extra loss and current profile keep them seventh.
  8. 8Tottenham Hotspur
    2-7 | -69 | John Long
    Tottenham owns the closest-game highlight of the month, but the broader record is still bottom-board material.
The One-Slot Problem

The race is fun because only one team goes up. Barcelona has earned the line. FL Fart has earned the argument. Inter has earned more attention than a third-place sentence usually gives. That is a good board, and it should stay volatile.
