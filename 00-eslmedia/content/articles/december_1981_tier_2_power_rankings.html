Europa League Power Rankings: Marseille Separates

Marseille was already dangerous in November; after a perfect December, Robert Parish and company have turned Tier 2 into a chase.

Nina ValeDecember 1981 power board

Last month, Sheffield United owned the first line because the differential was overwhelming. December gives the board a cleaner answer. Marseille is 13-1 overall, went 7-0 this sim, and now carries a plus-176 season profile. Robert Parish anchors the case, but the larger point is simpler: this is the one Europa League team winning like promotion is already late.

Europa League Board - Movement From November
  1. 1Marseille
    Up 1 | 13-1 | +176 | Robert Parish
    The perfect month makes this easy. Marseille has the record, the margin, and the cleanest promotion case in the league.
  2. 2Sheffield United
    Down 1 | 9-5 | +94 | Rickey Green
    Sheffield is still second because the full-season margin remains excellent. A 3-4 December just removes the right to be first.
  3. 3Benfica
    No change | 9-6 | +41 | Moses Malone
    Benfica is close enough to make the promotion race uncomfortable. The record is strong; the latest 4-4 run stops the jump.
  4. 4Juventus
    Up 1 | 8-6 | +52 | Kareem Abdul-Jabbar
    Juventus earns the rise with a 5-3 month and positive margin. The ceiling is not Marseille's, but the line is moving up.
  5. 5Chelsea
    Down 1 | 6-8 | +30 | Jack Sikma
    Chelsea remains the confusing team. A positive differential says there is quality here; a 2-5 December says that quality keeps arriving late.
  6. 6Ajax
    No change | 5-9 | -65 | Cliff Robinson
    Ajax at least stabilized after November's skid. The broader record still blocks a more generous ranking.
  7. 7Monaco
    No change | 4-9 | -151 | Purvis Short
    Monaco has more wins than Villa, but the minus-151 season margin keeps the floor frighteningly low.
  8. 8Aston Villa
    No change | 2-12 | -177 | Bill Laimbeer
    Villa finally won twice. That matters emotionally. It does not yet move them off the bottom of the board.
What Could Change

Sheffield can climb back quickly if the December dip was schedule noise. Chelsea is the uncertainty team because the margin case is better than the record. Marseille, for now, is the only team in Tier 2 that does not require a caveat.
